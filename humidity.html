Humidity

The correlation between latitude and humidity was relatively weak. Humidity levels were spread out relatively evenly across a wide range of latitudes with most cities recording a humdiity between 60-100% on January 24, 2020. A concentration of cities at higher latitudes recorded humidity levels near 100% which may be due to cities near large bodies of water like the Atlantic Ocean where humidity levels are generally higher.
